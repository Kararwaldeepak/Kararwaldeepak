## 👨‍🔬 Research Profile

My research focuses on the generation, manipulation, imaging, and propagation of broadband terahertz radiation. I investigate structured THz vector beams, spatio-spectral polarization states, spintronic THz emission, hollow-core metallic waveguides, and THz time-domain spectroscopy.

My work combines **experimental optics**, **electromagnetic simulations**, **signal processing**, and **scientific visualization** to study THz emission, propagation, polarization, dispersion, and light–matter interactions.

## 📐 THz Delay-Scan Relations

A THz scan records \(N\) images separated by a mechanical stage step \(\Delta L\). For an optical-path multiplier \(m\),

$$
\Delta t=\frac{m\Delta L}{c},
\qquad
L_{\mathrm{scan}}=(N-1)\Delta L,
\qquad
T_{\mathrm{span}}=(N-1)\Delta t.
$$

The frequency-domain sampling is determined by

$$
\Delta f=\frac{1}{N\Delta t},
\qquad
f_{\mathrm{Nyquist}}=\frac{1}{2\Delta t},
\qquad
N_f=\left\lfloor\frac{N}{2}\right\rfloor+1.
$$

The temporal pulse width and spectral bandwidth are

$$
\tau_{\mathrm{FWHM}}=t_2-t_1,
\qquad
B=f_{\mathrm{high}}-f_{\mathrm{low}}.
$$
